Is Spinning good for weight loss?

The high-intensity of spin class is an effective, efficient way to burn calories, and the pedaling gives you some resistance training, too. But if your only exercise, ever, is spinning, you'll need to add more resistance training, two or three times a week, if weight loss is your goal.
